Welcome

Welcome Stevens students! Are you itching to enjoy the outdoors but don't know where to go? If so, you've come to the right spot. Trail Blaze is a service to help Stevens students locate parks near Hoboken best suited to their needs. We provide a range of parks with different trail difficulties and lengths. We provide driving directions, possile transit routes and uber cost estimations to different parks in the area.
